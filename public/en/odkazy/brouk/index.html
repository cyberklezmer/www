My Beetle

Professor Tomáš Tichý is an entomologist alongside his main career, and he has named beetles he discovered after his colleagues. Honza Večeř has epicitus veceri, Miloš Kopa has leptura kopai, and I have microdebilissa smidi. I see it as something that one cannot influence which genus a newly discovered specimen belongs to.
